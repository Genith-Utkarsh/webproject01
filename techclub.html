@Geeks For Geeks


The Geeks for Geeks Club at VIT Pune is a community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to learning, innovating, and sharing knowledge. Our mission is to foster a culture of coding, collaboration, and creativity. 

OUR MOTIVE

  • Develop programming skills in various languages
  • Explore emerging technologies (AI, ML, IoT, etc.)
  • Participate in coding challenges and hackathons
  • Host workshops, webinars, and tech talks
  • Collaborate on open-source projects
