@Chess Club

The Chess Club at VIT Pune aims to provide a platform for students to develop their critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills through the game of chess. Our mission is to create a community of chess enthusiasts, promote the game, and participate in inter-collegiate and national tournaments. 

OUR MOTIVE

  • Improve strategic thinking and decision-making skills
  • Enhance problem-solving and analytical abilities
  • Develop critical thinking and logical reasoning
  • Participate in inter-collegiate and national chess tournaments
  • Foster a sense of community among chess enthusiasts



@Volleyball Club


The Volleyball Club at VIT Pune is a vibrant community of students passionate about volleyball. Our mission is to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship while competing at inter-collegiate and national levels. 

OUR MOTIVE

  • Develop volleyball skills and strategies
  • Enhance physical fitness and endurance
  • Foster teamwork and communication among players
  • Participate in inter-collegiate and national volleyball tournaments
  • Promote sportsmanship and fair play
